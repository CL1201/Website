FAQs

1.Why should I join a club or sport?

1. Joining a club or sport is a the most effective way to meet new people , develop new skills, and explore your interests outside of the classroom. It's a great opportunity to make friends who share your passions, boost your confidence, and even discover hidden talents. Additionally, it's a lot of fun and a great break from your studies!

2.How much time do clubs and sports take up?

2.The time commitment varies depending on the club or sport. Most clubs meet once, twice, or three times a week. Sports teams typically have a couple of practices and a game or two per week. Sports and Clubs understand you have academic commitments, so they aim to make schedules manageable. You'll find that the benefits of joining far outweigh the time invested!

3.What if I try a club or sport and don't like it?

3.That's perfectly okay! We encourage you to try things out and see what fits best. The goal is for you to find an activity you enjoy and feel good about. If a particular club or sport isn't for you, there are always other options to explore. Don't be afraid to try something new!
